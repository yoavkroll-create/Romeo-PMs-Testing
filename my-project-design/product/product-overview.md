# ReVerse

## Description
ReVerse is an AI platform that takes any uploaded song, transcribes it, analyzes its structure, and regenerates it in a completely different musical style. Users choose whether to keep their original vocals or replace them with an AI voice — then export a fully reimagined production in minutes.

## Problems & Solutions

### Problem 1: Genre Exploration Requires a Studio and a Budget
Hearing how a song sounds in a different genre means hiring a producer or spending days in a session. ReVerse lets anyone upload audio and receive a fully regenerated production in a new style — no studio time required.

### Problem 2: Artists Are Locked Into One Version of a Song
Once a track is released, exploring alternate arrangements or moods is impractical. ReVerse makes it instant — the same song can become lo-fi, cinematic, trap, or bossa nova in one click.

### Problem 3: Content Creators Need Fresh Musical Variety Fast
TikTok creators, agencies, and podcasters need genre-varied versions of audio content without original production resources. ReVerse provides export-ready outputs with social-ready short versions built in.

### Problem 4: Labels and Producers Have No Fast A/B Testing Workflow
Testing whether a song works better as an acoustic ballad or an EDM festival track takes weeks of studio work. ReVerse compresses that to minutes, enabling rapid creative experimentation.

## Key Features
- Upload MP3 or MP4 — audio is automatically separated into vocals and instrumentals
- AI transcription with tempo, key, and structure detection (verse / chorus / bridge)
- Genre preset library: Bossa Nova, Afrobeat, 90s Boom Bap, Cinematic Orchestra, Lo-fi Chill, EDM Festival, and more
- Custom style prompts: describe any mood, tempo, instrument, or vocal direction in plain text
- Dual vocal mode — keep the original vocal or regenerate with an AI voice
- Full AI re-harmonization, rhythm re-arrangement, and instrumentation regeneration
- Export as WAV / MP3, individual stems, or social-ready short clips
- Subscription tiers: Free (1 remix/month), Pro (unlimited + stems), Studio (AI voice cloning + commercial license)
