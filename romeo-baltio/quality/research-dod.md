# Research — Definition of Done

Applies to both Market Research (`/romeo-research`) and Deep Research (`/romeo-deep-research`).

## Market Research Criteria

| # | Criterion | Description |
|---|-----------|-------------|
| 1 | **Market overview has data** | Market size, trends, or growth indicators are cited — not just generic claims. |
| 2 | **Problem is validated** | External evidence confirms the problem exists and matters to the target users. |
| 3 | **Minimum 4 competitors analyzed** | At least 4 real, identifiable competitors are analyzed with strengths and weaknesses. |
| 4 | **Feature landscape covers 5+ categories** | The report identifies at least 5 major feature categories and how competitors approach each. |
| 5 | **Opportunity areas are specific** | Opportunities are concrete enough to inform feature decisions, not generic statements. |
| 6 | **Insights connect to Baseline** | Every key insight references how it relates to the Baseline Spec's problem, users, or capabilities. |
| 7 | **Sources are identified** | Research sources (tools used, data origins) are listed. |

## Deep Research Additional Criteria

| # | Criterion | Description |
|---|-----------|-------------|
| 8 | **Minimum 4 of 8 landscapes covered** | At least 4 research landscapes have been researched and documented. |
| 9 | **Assumptions validated/invalidated** | The report explicitly lists which Baseline assumptions were confirmed or contradicted by research. |
| 10 | **Strategic implications are actionable** | The strategic implications section provides specific, actionable recommendations for the Initial PRD. |

## Evaluation

- Market Research requires criteria 1–7.
- Deep Research requires criteria 1–10.
- Stage is complete when all applicable criteria pass.
